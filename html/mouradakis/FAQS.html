Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym's operating hours?

Our gym is open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM and Saturday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Premium members have 24/7 access.

What is included in the membership plans?

Each membership plan offers different benefits. The Basic Plan provides gym access during regular hours, the Standard Plan includes group classes, and the Premium Plan offers personal training, 24/7 access, and more. Check the "Membership Plans" section for details.

Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months in case of medical or personal reasons. Cancellations require a 30-day notice. Contact our support team for assistance.

Do you offer trial memberships?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for new members to explore our facilities and services before committing to a plan.

Are personal trainers available?

Yes, personal trainers are available for Premium Plan members or as an add-on service for other members. Our certified trainers will help you achieve your fitness goals.
