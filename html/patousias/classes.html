Our Classes

City Gym offers a variety of fitness classes to help you achieve your personal fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, we have something for everyone!

YOGA

Relax, breathe, and stretch your way to better health with our Yoga classes. Perfect for improving flexibility, posture, and mental wellness.

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner to Advanced
  • Benefits: Increased flexibility, stress reduction, improved mental focus.

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Get your heart racing and your metabolism going with our HIIT classes. A perfect combination of intense bursts of activity followed by short recovery periods.

  • Duration: 30 to 45 minutes
  • Difficulty: Intermediate to Advanced
  • Benefits: Increased cardiovascular fitness, fat burning, improved strength.

Strength Training

Focus on building muscle and increasing strength with a variety of weight lifting exercises and resistance training techniques.

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Difficulty: All levels
  • Benefits: Increased muscle mass, enhanced strength, better overall endurance.

Zumba

Dance your way to fitness with fun and energetic Zumba routines set to high-energy Latin music. A great cardio workout that doesn’t feel like exercise!

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Benefits: Weight loss, improved coordination, enhanced cardiovascular fitness.

Pilates

Strengthen your core, improve flexibility, and increase body awareness with our Pilates classes, designed to engage the mind and body.

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner to Advanced
  • Benefits: Core strength, improved posture, increased flexibility.

Kickboxing

Kickboxing is an intense workout that combines martial arts techniques with high-energy cardio. Perfect for improving strength, coordination, and agility.

  • Duration: 45 minutes
  • Difficulty: Intermediate to Advanced
  • Benefits: Increased strength, endurance, stress relief, improved coordination.

Spin

Get ready for a high-intensity cardio workout with our spin classes. Perfect for improving cardiovascular fitness and building leg strength.

  • Duration: 45 minutes
  • Difficulty: All levels
  • Benefits: Enhanced cardiovascular endurance, calorie burning, toning legs.

Barre

Barre classes combine ballet-inspired moves with elements of Pilates, dance, yoga, and strength training to give you a full-body workout.

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Benefits: Improved posture, flexibility, strength, and balance.

Boxing

Work on your cardio, strength, and agility with boxing classes designed to enhance your coordination and overall fitness.

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Difficulty: Beginner to Advanced
  • Benefits: Increased strength, stamina, agility, and stress relief.
