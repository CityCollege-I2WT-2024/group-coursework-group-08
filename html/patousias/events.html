Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the events?

Please bring comfortable workout attire, a water bottle, and a towel. For outdoor events, don't forget sunscreen and a hat!

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in as many events as you'd like! Just be sure to check the event timings and registration details.

Are there age restrictions for the events?

Our events are open to all ages, but certain activities may have specific age requirements. Check the event details for more information.
