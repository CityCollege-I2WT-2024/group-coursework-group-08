Additional Information

Our gym offers a variety of group fitness classes, including Yoga, HIIT, Spin, Zumba, Kickboxing, and more. Each class is designed to cater to various fitness levels, from beginners to advanced athletes. If you’re looking for personalized training, we also offer one-on-one sessions with our expert trainers.

Check the schedule for available classes, and don’t forget to book your spot in advance! Some classes may have limited space, and spots fill up quickly, especially for popular sessions like Spin and HIIT.
