Gym Equipment Guide

Treadmill

Use the treadmill for cardio workouts. Adjust the speed and incline to match your fitness level.

Instructions: Start with a 5-minute warm-up walk, gradually increase speed for a run, and end with a cool-down.

Dumbbells

Dumbbells are versatile for strength training. Use them for exercises like bicep curls, shoulder presses, and lunges.

Instructions: Select a comfortable weight and maintain proper form to avoid injuries.

Bench Press

The bench press targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It is ideal for upper body strength.

Instructions: Lie on the bench, grip the barbell evenly, and lower it to your chest before pushing back up.

Rowing Machine

A full-body cardio machine that targets the back, arms, and legs. Perfect for endurance training.

Instructions: Maintain a straight back, pull the handle to your chest, and extend your legs fully.

Leg Press

The leg press strengthens the lower body, including quads, hamstrings, and glutes.

Instructions: Adjust the seat, place your feet shoulder-width apart, and push the platform with control.

Pull-Up Bar

Perfect for upper body strength, focusing on back, shoulders, and arms.

Instructions: Grip the bar with palms facing away, pull yourself up until your chin is above the bar, and lower back down.
